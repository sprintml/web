Introducing the SprintML Lab
----------------------------

We are the SprintML lab with a research focus on Secure, Private, Robust, INterpretable, and Trustworthy Machine Learning. The lab is jointly led by Professors [Adam Dziedzic](https://adam-dziedzic.com/) & [Franziska Boenisch](https://franziska-boenisch.de/). Join SprintML!
--------------

We are currently hiring Ph.D. students, Postdocs, and Research Interns with a research focus in one or multiple of the following areas:
